Education, youth, culture and sport Council

Committee Description

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world” – Nelson Mandela

The Education, Youth, Culture and Sport Council will discuss and propose reforms for the challenges hindering youth development and education, that have been brought about by the pandemic. The central issues are, the well-being of Europe’s youth after months of isolation, and the evolution and access to digital education.
