Guillermo Bazán

European Council

Welcome Speech

Welcome

Dear delegates,

Please be welcome to the European Council of the new MUE ESEN 2020. My name is Guillermo Bazán, a Business Engineering student, and it is an honor to take the seat as the President of this Commission.

It is of great pleasure for me to have the opportunity to be a part of this educational experience, during which, our mission is to help you reap the most benefits along the way. The model’s objective is to promote the solution of problems through critical thinking and debate, a necessity in today’s ever-changing world. It is considerably important for us that this proves to be an experience where you are able to grow, learn, and expand your mind, in order to embrace different perspectives and stances. Furthermore, I hope that you are able to acquire skills that allow you to handle discussions, especially disagreements, in such a fashion that solutions may be found diplomatically.

The European Council is the European Union’s commission in charge of defining general political direction and priorities of the European Union. By being part of this amazing experience, we encounter with many different nations with a variety of political, social and economic perspectives about the world. By working together and getting to a mutual benefit, it is our duty to determine a solution to a real-world conflict that affects every one of us.

Nowadays, we, the young people, have the duty to change the world in a positive way. It doesn’t matter where we come from, our responsibility is to give something good to the society. This kind of activities let us begin in this journey of change, by putting into practice our critical thinking, leadership and negotiation.

I encourage you to give your best effort, as I firmly believe that hard work will lead you to superb achievements. My expectations lie on this commission being one of the most outstanding in the model. It is up to each and every one of us who are taking part in this activity to make it so. Best of luck delegates!
