Daniela Reyes

Education, youth, culture and sport Council

Welcome Speech

Dear delegates,

Welcome to the Education, Youth, Culture and Sport Council. This year I have the honor and privilege of presiding over this important committee. COVID-19 has ravaged economies and health care systems throughout the world. While topics such as medical research, unemployment, and politics, have been in the forefront throughout the year, other important issues have been pushed to the background. This includes education and youth development.

Today’s youth has been greatly affected by this pandemic, but its struggles have often been overshadowed by other topics. Therefore, it is the duty of this Council to ensure that their voices are heard. While young people are less likely to suffer grave medical complications from COVID-19, they have nonetheless been affected both mentally and physically by this pandemic. Education and youth development are the main points in our agenda, and we must make them a priority for Europe. The prosperity, happiness and progress of students throughout the continent lies in our hands.

This committee will be very active and dynamic. We are looking for creativity and passion in all delegates, since we’re all students and understand the obstacles today 's youth faces. The objective is to give you a platform to discuss a topic close to you, from the perspective of those who have the power to enact real changes in Europe's educational systems. I wish you all the best and I hope this topic inspires you.

-Daniela Reyes, President EYCS Council
